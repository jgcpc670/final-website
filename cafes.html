Café 670

A modern-industrial style cafe located across fishing base in Garapan

The interior

The cafe has an open concept layout, but the decor and warm atmosphere makes the overall feel, cozy.

My favorite beverage to get from here is the Green Tea Latte, or Matcha Latte.

Ohas Boulangerie

A french-style bakery in the heart of Garapan, originally located in Kensington Hotel, Ohas Boulangerie & Café in Garapan offers a wide array of freshly baked goods and handcrafted desserts, made by professional patisserie chefs

      Blueberry Danish

      Chocolate Croissant
